Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of the greatest scientists and leaders India has ever seen. He played a key role in India’s missile development programs and contributed immensely to the nation’s defense and space research.

What I admire most about Dr. Kalam is his simplicity, humility, and dedication to education. Despite achieving great success, he always remained grounded and inspired millions of students to dream big and work hard.

His thoughts on leadership, innovation, and youth empowerment continue to guide future generations. Dr. Kalam truly believed that education and knowledge are the strongest weapons to change the world.
